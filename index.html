What's this

Dedicated time to hack on the latest AI — with people who get it.

Don't just read about it. Don't just watch the YouTube review. Hack & Hang is about carving out real time to try things with friends who can actually help when you get stuck.

Try over talk

Vibe check

You'll fit right in if…

  • You use AI tools daily — not just ChatGPT, the weird stuff too
  • You've hacked something recently you're genuinely proud of
  • You're ambitious or deeply knowledgeable in your field
  • You have FOMO on the latest tools (everyone does, we just do something about it)
  • You'd rather try something for an hour than debate it for a week
